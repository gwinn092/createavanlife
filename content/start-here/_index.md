# Van Life Starter Checklist

If you are new, do these in order:

1. Choose a reliable van and reserve a maintenance buffer.
2. Build only essentials first: sleep, airflow, basic power, basic cooking.
3. Pick a simple power path: portable station now or full DIY later.
4. Test your routine for 30 days before buying comfort upgrades.
5. Use reviews with real tradeoffs, not just feature lists.
