**Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you purchase through them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.**

This is required by the FTC and we're glad to be upfront about it: Create A Van Life participates in affiliate programs including the Amazon Associates Program and others. When you click a clearly marked affiliate link and make a qualifying purchase, the retailer pays us a small referral fee. The price you pay does not change.

**What this means for our recommendations.** Affiliate income does not determine what we recommend. Our recovery-first framework exists because we actually live this way — eight years in a van, real loads, real conditions. We'd rather lose a commission than recommend gear that'll leave you stranded. Products we don't recommend don't get affiliate links.

**How to spot affiliate links.** Posts that contain affiliate links carry a short disclosure near the top of the page.

**Amazon Associates.** As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases made through Amazon links on this site.

**Other programs.** We may participate in affiliate programs for other retailers. Any such relationship is governed by the same standard: we only link to products we'd recommend regardless of commission.
