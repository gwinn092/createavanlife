Sizing your system starts with daily behavior, not product marketing. Track real device use first, then choose capacity.

## Practical Sizing Method

1. List daily loads (laptop, router, lights, fan, charging).
2. Estimate hours used per load.
3. Build a conservative daily total and add margin.
4. Match station size to both usage and recharge reality.

> Diagram placeholder: Daily load worksheet and margin model.

## Real-Life Example

A remote worker with laptop + hotspot + lighting usually needs less total capacity than expected, but needs faster recharge than expected.

## Where Most Buyers Misjudge

Buyers often chase maximum Wh and ignore charge recovery. In van life, slow recovery creates stress faster than slightly smaller capacity.
