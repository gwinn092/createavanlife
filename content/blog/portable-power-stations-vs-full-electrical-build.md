This decision controls budget, build timeline, and failure risk.

## Power Station Wins If

- You need power quickly.
- Your loads are moderate.
- You prefer lower install risk.

## DIY System Wins If

- You run high-watt appliances daily.
- You want deep integration and maximum control.
- You can design safe electrical systems or hire help.

## Practical Recommendation

Start with a quality station, then move to fixed electrical when your daily usage data proves you need it.
