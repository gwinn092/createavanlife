Starting van life on a budget is mostly about sequencing. People overspend when they buy comfort upgrades before core systems.

## Phase 1: Buy the Right Van

A reliable used van with service history beats a newer van you cannot afford to maintain. Budget for an inspection and first-month repairs.

## Phase 2: Build Essentials Only

Start with sleep, airflow, basic power, and simple cooking. Skip expensive extras until you test your routine for at least 30 days.

## Phase 3: Pick a Simple Power Path

If you are unsure, start with a power station and scale later. See [Portable Power Stations Under $500](/best-of/power-stations-under-500/).

## Phase 4: Track Monthly Costs

Fuel, food, and maintenance matter more than one-time build costs over time.
