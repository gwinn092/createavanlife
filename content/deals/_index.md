## What to Check Before Buying

Before acting on a deal, run this checklist:

- Is the capacity right for my real daily loads — not my ideal-day estimate?
- Have I completed the **[Buyer Checklist](/fast-charging-power-station-buyer-checklist/)** for this purchase?
- Am I buying because this gear fits my situation, or because it is on sale?

If the answer to that last question is mostly "it is on sale," wait. A discounted unit that does not fit your recovery routine is still the wrong unit.

## Price Drift Warning

Power station prices move frequently. A price that looks like a deal today may have been the standard price last month, or the same price may return next week without a sale label.
