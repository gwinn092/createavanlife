## What "time to recover" means

Time to recover is the hours needed to refill enough usable energy for normal use after a rough day. We label related statements as:

- **Spec:** manufacturer charging inputs/timing claims.
- **Reported:** consistent third-party or owner patterns.
- **Measured:** direct CAVL observation.
- **Estimate:** modeled planning math.

## Why this beats raw Wh

Big batteries with slow refill often increase stress in real van life. A smaller unit with faster refill can recover your week sooner, especially when outlet access is short.
