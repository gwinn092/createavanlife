## Pros

- Layered approach can improve thermal performance in mixed climates.
- Materials are generally accessible and budget-scalable.
- Good fit for phased DIY builds.

## Cons

- Install complexity around curves and thermal bridging.
- Results depend heavily on air sealing quality.

## Who It’s For

- DIY builders prioritizing practical comfort over luxury finishes.
- People converting in variable climate conditions.

## Still Worth It?

Yes, when executed carefully. Material choice alone does not fix poor install strategy.

## Best Current Alternatives

- Simpler closed-cell foam strategies where labor time is constrained.
- Hybrid acoustic + thermal systems depending on noise goals.
