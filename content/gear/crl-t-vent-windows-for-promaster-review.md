## Pros

- Major improvement to crossflow and comfort.
- Better fresh-air management in hot conditions.
- Works well with roof fan systems.

## Cons

- Install requires confidence and precision cutting.
- Cost can feel high when combined with fan upgrades.

## Who It’s For

- Builders in warm/humid climates.
- Anyone frustrated by stagnant cabin air.

## Still Worth It?

Yes, especially when paired with a quality roof fan.

## Best Current Alternatives

- Non-vent side windows (lower cost, lower airflow).
- Portable airflow compromises when cutting is not possible.
